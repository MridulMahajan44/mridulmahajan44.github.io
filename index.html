Hi! I am a visiting scholar in the Machine Teaching Group at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems (MPI-SWS), advised by Dr. Adish Singla.

In the past, I have worked on neural combinatorial optimization as an intern in the Decision and Operation Technologies Group at Universität Bielefeld, advised by Prof. Dr. Kevin Tierney. I am also fortunate to have worked with (Late) Prof. Pramod Subramanyan on HyperLTL model checking.

I recently graduated with a (Dual Degree) B.Tech. + M.Tech. in Information Technology from the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Allahabad, India. There, I worked with Prof. G. C. Nandi on antipodal robotic grasping.

I am broadly interested in machine learning, reinforcement learning, and robotics, especially in reinforcement learning research grounded in robot learning applications.
